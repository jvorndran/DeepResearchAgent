## 1. Credit Cycle & Liquidity Analysis
These questions test the agent's ability to calculate spreads and identify "cracks" in the financial system.

* **Prompt:** *"Calculate the 12-month rolling correlation between High Yield Corporate Bond Spreads and a major bank index. How does this correlation shift during periods where bank tightening standards for C&I loans exceed 20%?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Analyze the 'Financial Conditions Impulse.' Retrieve a national financial conditions index and compare its 3-month rate of change against the forward P/E ratio of a broad equity index. Does a tightening impulse historically lead equity drawdowns by more than 2 quarters?"*

## 2. Inflation & Monetary Policy Efficacy
These focus on the "Real" vs "Nominal" gap, which is crucial for valuation models.

* **Prompt:** *"Decompose the current Consumer Price Index (CPI). Pull the Sticky-Price CPI and compare it to the Flexible-Price CPI. Based on the last 6 months of data, is the 'disinflation' narrative driven by volatile components or structural shifts in the service economy?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Calculate the 'Real Fed Funds Rate' by subtracting expected inflation from the effective federal funds rate. Cross-reference this with a recession indicator to identify if the current 'Real' rate is at a restrictive level historically associated with labor market cooling."*
* **Prompt:** *"Compare long-term forward inflation expectations against the 10-year breakeven inflation rate. If the forward expectation is significantly higher than the 10-year breakeven, what does this 'De-anchoring' signal suggest about the market's confidence in the long-term inflation target?"*

## 3. The "Yield Curve" & Macro Forecasting
Instead of just asking for the curve, ask for the *timing* of the inversion vs. the trough.

* **Prompt:** *"Retrieve the 10-Year vs 2-Year Treasury yield spread. Identify every instance of 're-steepening' after an inversion since 1980. What is the median lead time between the curve returning to positive territory and the start of a defined recession?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Analyze the 'Term Premium' proxy. Pull the 10-Year and 2-Year Treasury yields. Calculate the butterfly spread $(10Y - 5Y) - (5Y - 2Y)$ and explain what the current curvature suggests about market expectations for a 'soft landing' vs. 'hard landing'."*
* **Prompt:** *"Retrieve the 10-Year vs 3-Month Treasury yield spread and an excess bond premium proxy. Compare the predictive power of the yield curve vs. credit risk premiums for recession onset over the next 12 months."*

## 4. Labor Market & Consumer Health
Crucial for Retail and Consumer Discretionary sector coverage.

* **Prompt:** *"Compare the Personal Saving Rate against total consumer credit. Is the current consumer spending being funded by organic income growth or by a rapid expansion in revolving credit? Provide the debt-to-savings ratio trend over the last 24 months."*
* **Prompt:** *"Create a 'Labor Market Tightness Index' by dividing job openings by the number of unemployed persons. At what level has this ratio historically peaked before a meaningful rise in unemployment insurance claims?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Analyze real disposable personal income against consumer sentiment. Identify 'Divergence Zones' where sentiment is falling despite rising real income. Does this divergence typically precede a contraction in real personal consumption expenditures?"*

## 5. Equity Sector Rotation & Asset Allocation
These prompts bridge macro shifts to specific investment strategies and sector performance.

* **Prompt:** *"Retrieve the 10-Year Real Interest Rate and a major technology index. Calculate the rolling 6-month correlation between changes in real yields and the price-to-earnings multiple of the index. During which historical regimes did this correlation 'break', and what does the current real rate level imply for the 'valuation ceiling' of long-duration growth assets?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Analyze the 'Defensive vs. Cyclical' spread. Pull the performance of Consumer Staples and Consumer Discretionary sectors. Compare their relative performance ratio against a manufacturing PMI proxy. Does the current ratio suggest the market is pricing in a 'Recession' or a 'Mid-Cycle Acceleration'?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Retrieve High Yield Corporate Bond Spreads and a small-cap equity index. Calculate the 'Credit-to-Equity Divergence' over the last 12 months. If credit spreads are widening while small-caps are rising, what does this historical anomaly suggest about the sustainability of the current rally in 'junk'-rated equities?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Compare the gold price against the 10-year real interest rate. Calculate the 'Real Rate Adjusted Gold Price' (residual analysis). If gold is trading at a significant premium to its historical real-rate correlation, what does this imply about 'Central Bank Diversification' vs. 'Inflation Hedging' demand?"*

## 6. Global Macro & FX Implications
These prompts test the agent's ability to analyze the 'Dollar Smile' and cross-currency carry trades.

* **Prompt:** *"Retrieve a major US dollar index and the yield spread between the US and another major economy. Analyze the correlation between the exchange rate and the yield differential over the last 5 years. Does the current 'Carry Trade' unwind historical pattern suggest a volatility spike in equities?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Compare a trade-weighted US dollar index against a broad equity index. Identify 'Strong Dollar Regimes' (more than 2 standard deviations above mean). Does the US dollar's strength historically serve as a headwind for index earnings growth, and what is the current revenue-weighted sensitivity to a 'Dollar Breakout'?"*
* **Prompt:** *"Analyze the 'Emerging Markets Risk-On/Risk-Off' indicator. Retrieve an emerging markets index and a volatility index. Calculate the beta of EM equities to volatility during periods of liquidity tightening (measured by money supply growth rates below 5%)."*
