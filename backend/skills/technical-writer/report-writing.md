# Report Rules

1. **Analytical Tone:** Use specific statistics in parentheticals (e.g., `(r=0.82, p<0.01)`).
2. **Frameworks:** Discuss trends, correlations, or sector positioning.
3. **Sections (Order):**
   - ## Executive Summary (dense, 2-3 sentences)
   - ## Research Query (original text)
   - ## Data Sources (source, ticker, dates)
   - [Analysis Sections]
   - ## Methodology
   - ## Limitations
   - ## Disclaimer (must contain "financial advice" and "Past performance")
